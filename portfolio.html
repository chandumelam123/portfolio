Modes Of Transport

Bicycle

A bicycle, also called a cycle or bike, is a human-powered, pedal-driven, single-track vehicle

Motor Cycle

A motor vehicle is a self-propelled vehicle, commonly wheeled, that does not operate on rails

Car

A car (or automobile) is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation.

Train

A train is a form of transport consisting of a series of connected vehicles
